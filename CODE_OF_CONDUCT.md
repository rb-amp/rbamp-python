# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

The rbAmp community welcomes contributions and discussions from anyone interested в open AC energy monitoring. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, или identity.

## Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Technical, constructive feedback
- Patience with newcomers (someone always knows less than you, и that's OK)
- Acknowledgment of others' work
- Respectful disagreement based on evidence

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, или personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or content
- Trolling, baiting, или deliberate disruption
- Doxxing или sharing private information without consent
- Spamming, advertising unrelated products
- Off-topic political discussions

## Scope

This Code applies to:
- All rb-amp GitHub repos (Issues, PRs, Discussions)
- forum.rbamp.com (when launched)
- rbAmp Discord server (when launched)
- Any space where you represent the rbAmp community

## Enforcement

Reports: **conduct@rbamp.com** (or hello@rbamp.com if conduct@ alias not yet active)

We will:
1. Review reports within 72 hours
2. Take action proportional to violation: warning → temporary ban → permanent ban
3. Not retaliate against good-faith reporters

This Code of Conduct is inspired by [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), adapted for the rbAmp project.
